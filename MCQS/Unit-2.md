## 📚 Section A: Basic Concepts (1–15)

### 1. An ecosystem is:
- a) Only living organisms
- b) Only non-living components

- d) Only plants

**✅ Answer:** c) Interaction between living and non-living components

---

### 2. Producers are:
- a) Animals
- b) Plants
- c) Humans
- d) Decomposers

**✅ Answer:** b) Plants

---

### 3. Consumers are:

- b) Producers

- d) Decomposers

**✅ Answer:** c) Heterotrophs

---

### 4. Decomposers include:
- a) Plants
- b) Animals
- c) Bacteria and fungi

**✅ Answer:** c) Bacteria and fungi

---

- a) Water
