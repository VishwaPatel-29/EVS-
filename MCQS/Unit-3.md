## 📝 Quick Revision

| Topic | Key Point |
|---------|------------|
| Natural Resources | Materials obtained from nature |
| Renewable Resources | Can be replenished naturally |
| Non-renewable Resources | Limited and exhaustible |
| Biotic Resources | Obtained from living organisms |
| Abiotic Resources | Obtained from non-living components |
| Deforestation | Large-scale cutting of forests |
| Afforestation | Planting trees in barren areas |
| Rainwater Harvesting | Collection and storage of rainwater |
| Groundwater Depletion | Excessive extraction of underground water |
| Drip Irrigation | Most water-efficient irrigation method |
| Green Revolution | Increased food production using modern techniques |
| Organic Farming | Uses natural methods of cultivation |
| Fossil Fuels | Coal, petroleum, and natural gas |
| Renewable Energy | Solar, wind, biomass, hydropower |
| Geothermal Energy | Heat energy from Earth's interior |
| Biomass Energy | Energy from plant and animal wastes |
| 3R Principle | Reduce, Reuse, Recycle |
| Sustainable Development | Meeting present needs without compromising future generations |
| Carbon Sequestration | Absorption and storage of atmospheric carbon dioxide |
| Resource Conservation | Efficient and responsible use of resources |
