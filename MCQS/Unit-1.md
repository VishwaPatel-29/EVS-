## 📚 Section 1: Basic Concepts

### 1. Environmental Science deals with:
- a) Only plants
- b) Only animals
- c) Interaction between living and non-living components
- d) Only pollution

**✅ Answer:** c) Interaction between living and non-living components

---

### 2. Which is a biotic component?
- a) Air
- b) Soil
- c) Plant
- d) Water

**✅ Answer:** c) Plant

---

### 3. Which is an abiotic component?
- a) Bacteria
- b) Fungi
- c) Water
- d) Animals

**✅ Answer:** c) Water

---

### 4. The largest component of atmosphere is:
- a) Oxygen
- b) Nitrogen
- c) Carbon dioxide
- d) Hydrogen

**✅ Answer:** b) Nitrogen

---

### 5. Hydrosphere includes:
- a) Land
- b) Air
- c) Water
- d) Plants

**✅ Answer:** c) Water

---

### 6. Lithosphere refers to:
- a) Water
- b) Air
- c) Land
- d) Life

**✅ Answer:** c) Land

---

### 7. Biosphere includes:
- a) Only plants
- b) Only animals
- c) All living organisms
- d) Only microorganisms

**✅ Answer:** c) All living organisms

---

### 8. Environmental Science is:
- a) Single subject
- b) Multidisciplinary subject
- c) Only biology
- d) Only chemistry

**✅ Answer:** b) Multidisciplinary subject

---

### 9. Which of the following is not a natural component?
- a) Air
- b) Water
- c) Plastic
- d) Soil

**✅ Answer:** c) Plastic

---

### 10. Which gas is essential for breathing?
- a) Nitrogen
- b) Oxygen
- c) Carbon dioxide
- d) Helium

**✅ Answer:** b) Oxygen

---

### 11. Which sphere supports life?
- a) Atmosphere
- b) Lithosphere
- c) Biosphere
- d) Hydrosphere

**✅ Answer:** c) Biosphere

---

### 12. Soil belongs to:
- a) Hydrosphere
- b) Atmosphere
- c) Lithosphere
- d) Biosphere

**✅ Answer:** c) Lithosphere

---

### 13. Sunlight is an example of:
- a) Biotic component
- b) Abiotic component
- c) Living component
- d) Organic component

**✅ Answer:** b) Abiotic component

---

### 14. The environment consists of:
- a) Only living things
- b) Only non-living things
- c) Both living and non-living
- d) Only water

**✅ Answer:** c) Both living and non-living

---

### 15. Microorganisms are:
- a) Abiotic
- b) Biotic
- c) Non-living

**✅ Answer:** b) Biotic

### 16. Sustainable development means:
- a) Rapid use of resources
- b) No use of resources
- c) Use without harming future generations

---

---

- a) Atmosphere

---

### 19. Air pollution mainly affects:
- a) Soil

---

### 20. Water pollution affects:

---

### 21. Deforestation leads to:

---

### 22. Industrialization causes:

## 📌 Additional Important MCQs

- **World Environment Day is celebrated on:**  
  **✅ Answer:** 5 June

- **The study of organisms and their environment is called:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Ecology

- **The branch of science that studies weather and climate is:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Meteorology

- **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) is conducted to:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Predict environmental consequences of development projects

- **The Brundtland Commission defined:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Sustainable Development

- **Using renewable energy sources is an example of:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Sustainable Development

- **Rapid industrialization often leads to:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Environmental degradation

- **Urbanization contributes to:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Environmental pollution

- **The atmosphere protects Earth from:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Harmful ultraviolet radiation

- **Demography helps Environmental Science understand:**  
  **✅ Answer:** Population trends
