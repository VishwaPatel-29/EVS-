## ❓ 1. Define Environmental Science.

### ✅ Answer
Environmental Science is a multidisciplinary branch of science that studies the relationship between living organisms and their surroundings. It deals with environmental problems, their causes, effects, and solutions. It also helps in the conservation of natural resources and environmental protection.

---

## ❓ 2. State the scope of Environmental Science.

### ✅ Answer
The scope of Environmental Science is very broad. It includes the study of natural resources, ecosystems, biodiversity, pollution control, waste management, environmental conservation, and sustainable development. It helps in understanding environmental issues and finding effective solutions.

---

## ❓ 3. What is meant by environment?

### ✅ Answer
Environment refers to everything that surrounds and influences living organisms. It includes both living (biotic) components such as plants and animals, and non-living (abiotic) components such as air, water, soil, and sunlight. These components interact with each other to support life on Earth.

---

## ❓ 4. Name the four major components of the environment.

### ✅ Answer

| Component | Description |
|------------|------------|
| 🌤️ Atmosphere | Layer of gases surrounding the Earth |
| 🌊 Hydrosphere | All water bodies present on Earth |
| ⛰️ Lithosphere | Solid outer crust of the Earth |
| 🌱 Biosphere | Region where living organisms exist |

Together, these components support and sustain life on Earth.

---

## ❓ 5. Define Atmosphere.

### ✅ Answer
The atmosphere is the gaseous envelope surrounding the Earth. It is mainly composed of nitrogen and oxygen along with other gases. It provides oxygen for respiration, regulates temperature, and protects living organisms from harmful ultraviolet radiation.

---

## ❓ 6. Define Hydrosphere.

### ✅ Answer
The hydrosphere includes all forms of water present on Earth, such as oceans, rivers, lakes, glaciers, groundwater, and water vapor. It plays a vital role in supporting life and maintaining climatic conditions.

---

## ❓ 7. What is Lithosphere?

### ✅ Answer
The lithosphere is the solid outer layer of the Earth consisting of rocks, minerals, and soil. It forms the land surface and provides essential natural resources required for human activities and agriculture.

---

## ❓ 8. What is Biosphere?

### ✅ Answer
The biosphere is the part of the Earth where life exists. It includes all living organisms and their interactions with air, water, and land. It is known as the life-supporting zone of the Earth.

---

## ❓ 9. Mention any two functions of the atmosphere.

### ✅ Answer

✔️ Provides oxygen required for respiration.

✔️ Protects the Earth from harmful ultraviolet rays of the Sun.

Additionally, it helps regulate temperature and weather conditions.

---

## ❓ 10. Why is environmental awareness important?

### ✅ Answer
Environmental awareness helps people understand environmental problems such as pollution, deforestation, and climate change. It encourages the conservation of natural resources and promotes eco-friendly practices for a sustainable future.

---

## ❓ 11. What is meant by sustainable development?

### ✅ Answer
Sustainable development refers to development that meets the needs of the present generation without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It aims to balance economic growth, environmental protection, and social well-being.

---

## ❓ 12. Explain the interdisciplinary nature of Environmental Science.

### ✅ Answer
Environmental Science is interdisciplinary because it combines knowledge from various fields such as biology, chemistry, physics, geology, economics, and social sciences. This integrated approach helps in understanding and solving environmental issues effectively.

---

## ❓ 13. List any two environmental issues caused by industrialization.

### ✅ Answer

### 🏭 Air Pollution
Industries release harmful gases and smoke into the atmosphere, causing air pollution.

### 💧 Water Pollution
Industrial waste discharged into rivers and lakes contaminates water bodies and harms aquatic life.

---

## ❓ 14. What is environmental degradation?

### ✅ Answer
Environmental degradation refers to the deterioration of the natural environment due to activities such as pollution, deforestation, overexploitation of resources, and industrialization. It negatively affects ecosystems and human life.

---

## ❓ 15. Differentiate between natural and man-made environments.

### ✅ Answer

| 🌿 Natural Environment | 🏙️ Man-Made Environment |
|----------------------|----------------------|
| Created by nature | Created by humans |
| Includes forests, rivers, mountains, and oceans | Includes buildings, roads, industries, and cities |
| Exists naturally | Developed and modified by humans |
| Supports biodiversity | Designed for human needs |

**Example:** A forest is a natural environment, whereas a city is a man-made environment.
