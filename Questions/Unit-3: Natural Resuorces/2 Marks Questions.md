# 🌍 Environmental Science (EVS)
## 📚 Unit 3: Natural Resources
### 📝 Short Questions (2 Marks Each)

---

# ❓ Question 1
## Define natural resources.

### ✅ Answer

Natural resources are materials and substances that occur naturally in the environment and are useful to humans. These resources are obtained from nature and used to fulfill human needs.

### 🌱 Examples:
- Water
- Forests
- Minerals
- Air
- Soil

---

# ❓ Question 2
## What are forest resources?

### ✅ Answer

Forest resources are the various products and benefits obtained from forests. They include timber, fuelwood, medicinal plants, fruits, and wildlife habitats.

### 🌳 Examples:
- Timber
- Bamboo
- Firewood
- Medicinal herbs

---

# ❓ Question 3
## State any two uses of forests.

### ✅ Answer

Forests provide numerous benefits to humans and the environment.

### 🌲 Two Uses of Forests:
1. Provide timber and fuelwood.
2. Prevent soil erosion and maintain ecological balance.

---

# ❓ Question 4
## What is water conservation?

### ✅ Answer

Water conservation is the practice of using water efficiently and protecting water resources from wastage and pollution to ensure their availability for future generations.

---

# ❓ Question 5
## Mention any two causes of water scarcity.

### ✅ Answer

### 💧 Causes of Water Scarcity:
1. Overuse and wastage of water resources.
2. Irregular rainfall and drought conditions.

---

# ❓ Question 6
## Define mineral resources.

### ✅ Answer

Mineral resources are naturally occurring inorganic substances found in the Earth's crust that are extracted and used for industrial and economic purposes.

---

# ❓ Question 7
## Give two examples of mineral resources.

### ✅ Answer

### ⛏️ Examples:
1. Iron Ore
2. Bauxite

Other examples include copper, gold, limestone, and mica.

---

# ❓ Question 8
## What are food resources?

### ✅ Answer

Food resources are the sources from which humans obtain food for survival and nutrition. They include agricultural crops, livestock, fisheries, and forest products.

### 🌾 Examples:
- Rice
- Wheat
- Fish
- Milk

---

# ❓ Question 9
## Define agricultural sustainability.

### ✅ Answer

Agricultural sustainability refers to farming practices that meet present food needs without degrading natural resources, ensuring that future generations can also meet their food requirements.

---

# ❓ Question 10
## What is renewable energy?

### ✅ Answer

Renewable energy is energy obtained from natural sources that are continuously replenished and do not get exhausted through use.

### 🌞 Examples:
- Solar Energy
- Wind Energy

---

# ❓ Question 11
## Give two examples of renewable energy sources.

### ✅ Answer

### ♻️ Examples of Renewable Energy Sources:

1. Solar Energy ☀️
2. Wind Energy 🌬️

Other examples include hydroelectric energy and biomass energy.

---

# ❓ Question 12
## What are non-renewable energy resources?

### ✅ Answer

Non-renewable energy resources are energy sources that exist in limited quantities and cannot be replenished within a short period of time after use.

---

# ❓ Question 13
## Give two examples of non-renewable energy sources.

### ✅ Answer

### 🛢️ Examples:

1. Coal
2. Petroleum

Other examples include natural gas and nuclear fuels.

---

# ❓ Question 14
## Define conservation of natural resources.

### ✅ Answer

Conservation of natural resources refers to the protection, proper management, and wise use of natural resources to prevent depletion and ensure their availability for future generations.

---

# ❓ Question 15
## What is sustainable use of resources?

### ✅ Answer

Sustainable use of resources means utilizing natural resources in a responsible and efficient manner so that present needs are met without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.
